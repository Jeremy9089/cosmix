Who are Promo 2023?

We are a group of young people who started the ¡Supèrate! experience in 2020. And we strive every day to meet all our proposed goals and transform our lives. Our path has had many trials and difficulties, but nevertheless, we have been able to overcome them, because we always had the help of God and many special people around us.

OUR VALUES ARE

During these 3 years, we have learned many things in English and IT, but undoubtedly something that defines us are the values that we have learned and put into practice

Overcoming

We are students who demonstrate the ability to face and overcome challenges, displaying resilience and determination in their academic and personal pursuits.

Leadership

Excellent students take initiative and inspire others, effectively guiding their peers towards success and positively impacting their school or community.

Excellence

Students consistently strive for and achieve high standards of performance, going above and beyond expectations in their studies and extracurricular activities

Integrity

Excellent students uphold ethical principles and demonstrate honesty and fairness in all aspects of their academic and personal lives, acting as role models for others.

Solidarity

We are students who show empathy and actively engage in supporting and collaborating with their classmates and community members, fostering a sense of unity and inclusivity.

Innovation

Excellent students are creative thinkers who seek and implement new ideas and solutions, seeking innovative approaches to address challenges and improve their learning environment.
